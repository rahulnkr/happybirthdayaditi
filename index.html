Of all the days of the year, all 365 of them- today is your birthday?
Congratulations! What a spectacular thing.
Even before they are born, a newborn can figure out and reason why it is time to leave the nest.
Even if there is some misunderstanding and crying, it still happens! What a tiny brain a baby has!
Happy birthday, betu. Be proud of who you’re you. You're the egg who survived the miserable egg existence and grew into a person.
Do things you enjoy doing on this special day.
Because, hey, no matter how old you are, today is the final day you'll ever turn. The year after that will be different, and so on. Tomorrow will be different as well, as you will be a year older.
It's something you should welcome. It only happens once a year, and you are the only one who should be concerned because it is your time.
One of my favorite quotes is, "At least once in your life, express gratitude to the people who made your life worth living."
Therefore,
Thank you for waking me early in the mornings when I had my important interviews.
Thank you for being more excited than me in my ventures, and more sad than me in my failures.
Thank you for extending a helping hand when all the others wriggled out, and thank you for letting me raise my hopes on you without giving me a taste of that familiar sea of disappointment.
Thank you for showing that the greatest way to live our lives is to constantly see the bright side of things, appreciate our blessings, do our best, and never lose hope.
Thank you for demonstrating that exceptions may be found in any situation.
Thank you for being my confidante, my shoulder, and my 'that is where I go when I fall down' person.
Thank you for allowing yourself to be who you are. Because I honestly believe there aren't enough adjectives to adequately describe you.
